Privacy Policy

Effective Date:

1. No Personal Data Collection

PulseStream Preview does not collect, store, or share any personal data. No account, sign-up, email, phone number, or location data is required or collected.

2. No Tracking or Analytics

The app does not use analytics tools, tracking technologies, advertising SDKs, or cookies of any kind.

3. Music Preview Functionality

The app provides short audio previews through third-party services strictly for discovery purposes. PulseStream Preview does not host, own, or distribute any music content.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses publicly available APIs to retrieve music metadata, artwork, and preview audio. These third-party services may process requests in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Local Storage Only

  • Saved songs
  • Playlists
  • User preferences

This data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted externally.

6. No Downloads

The app does not provide full song downloads or permanent storage of third-party audio content.

7. Children’s Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Security

Since no personal data is collected, there is no risk of personal data exposure.

9. Updates

This policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes or improvements.
