About

PulseStream Preview is a lightweight music discovery app designed to help you explore songs quickly through short audio previews. No accounts, no clutter, just fast and clean playback.

Features

  • Instant playback of song previews
  • Fast and responsive search
  • Trending and recommended tracks
  • Save your favorite songs
  • Minimal and clean interface
