# Privacy Policy for PulseStream

**Effective Date:** April 2026

PulseStream ("the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

## 1. Information We Collect

PulseStream does not collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information.

You are not required to create an account, and the App does not collect data such as your name, email address, phone number, or precise location.

## 2. Music Preview Functionality

PulseStream allows users to search and play short audio previews of songs. These previews are provided by third-party services and are intended for preview purposes only.

PulseStream does not host or own any music content.

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses publicly available third-party APIs to retrieve music metadata, artwork, and preview audio.

These third-party services may process requests in accordance with their own privacy policies. PulseStream does not control or assume responsibility for how these third parties handle data.

## 4. Local Data Storage

PulseStream may store limited data locally on your device, such as:
- saved songs  
- playlists  
- user preferences  

This data is stored only on your device and is not transmitted to any external servers.

## 5. Downloads

PulseStream does not provide full song downloads or permanent storage of third-party audio content. Any playback is limited to preview content.

## 6. Analytics and Tracking

PulseStream does not use analytics tools, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or cookies to collect user data.

## 7. Children’s Privacy

PulseStream does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since no personal data is collected, the App is safe for general audiences.

## 8. Data Security

Since PulseStream does not collect or transmit personal data, there is no risk of user data being stored or exposed by the App.

## 9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
